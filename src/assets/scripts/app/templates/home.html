How football and friendship got people fighting
Watch from the beginning

This is the interactive story of a battalion that was raised during World War One in Edinburgh.

It was a brotherhood of sportsmen, formed by some of the best footballers in Scotland at the time. It is also a story of the women left behind, who worked in munitions factories, formed their own football teams and whose lives changed forever. Watch from the beginning.
